Paint Protection Film (PPF)

XPEL Film 10 Year Warranty

Protect your paint from chips, scratches, and road debris with coverage that matches how you drive.

Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a clear, flexible film that takes the hit from rock chips, road rash, and everyday abrasion. It’s designed for high‑impact areas like the bumper, hood, fenders, mirrors, and door edges. PPF is ideal if you drive highways often, commute year‑round, or want maximum protection on a newer vehicle. We install the film with clean edges and proper alignment so it looks natural and stays secure over time. Coverage can be targeted (partial) or expanded to a full‑front or full‑body install depending on your budget and goals. We use XPEL film options with a 10‑year warranty for long‑term confidence. Before installation, panels are cleaned and prepped so the film lays flat and the finish looks consistent. PPF can be paired with ceramic coating for easier washing and a deeper gloss on top of the film. Start a quote and we’ll help you choose the best coverage for your vehicle and the roads you drive.
